Microsoft Partner Resources

What are Microsoft Partner Resources?

The Microsoft Partners Resources repository consists of documents, links, and other assets designed to aid partners in developing practices, upskilling, certifications, and taking their solutions to market. Currently, this repository houses our Learning Plan Resources, with more sections to be added in the future.

Microsoft GPS Strategy

Microsoft Global Partner Solutions (GPS) is responsible for empowering partners to make more possible and transforming our US partner ecosystem into a strategic asset to drive Digital Transformation.
We're focused on:

  • Increasing profitable growth
  • Enhancing our partners' experience
  • Deepening the culture of partnering throughout the US subsidiary and Microsoft
